Leadership

Guided by access. Driven by people.

The Practx leadership team blends clinical expertise, operational rigor, and human-centered design to make dental care more connected for everyone.

We build momentum by listening to practices, investing in communities, and aligning every decision to our promise: access above all.

Team Practx

Meet the leaders advancing connected care.

Leadership principles

How we make decisions at Practx.

Access above all. Every initiative must expand who can receive or deliver dental care.

Measure what matters. We use data to elevate patient experiences, not to replace human relationships.

Build community. Partnerships with practices, educators, and entrepreneurs keep Practx grounded in real needs.

Lead with empathy. We seek first to understand before we design, deploy, or decide.

Advisory network

Voices from every corner of dentistry.

Practx partners with dental assistants, hygienists, practice owners, educators, and equipment technicians to refine every release. Quarterly advisory councils ensure we hear directly from people who keep operatories running and patients smiling.
