Why Practx

What it’s like to work here.

Mission with momentum. Every role contributes to expanding dental access. We celebrate wins that move patients, providers, and partners forward.

Remote-first flexibility. Teams collaborate across time zones with hub weeks in Austin for deep planning and connection.

Inclusive benefits. Competitive healthcare, wellness stipends, parental leave, and professional development budgets keep our people supported.

Current focus areas

We’re growing across product, clinical, and service teams.

Engineering & Product

Help build the Practx platform that powers scheduling, outreach, and equipment intelligence. Experience with healthcare integrations or Azure ecosystems is a plus.

Clinical Enablement

Partner with dental teams to activate best practices, training programs, and patient journeys that improve access.

Service & Support

Growth & Community

Tell the Practx story, support events, and expand partnerships that bring oral health access to new communities.
